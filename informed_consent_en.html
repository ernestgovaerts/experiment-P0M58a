Title of the research

Composition and spatial layout in natural and man-made images

Name + contact details supervisor and researcher(s):

Goal and methodology of the research
In this experiment, we will explore how people evaluate different types of images. These images include natural scenes and man-made objects. Your task is to rate each image based on various properties, such as complexity, composition, and how pleasant you find it. At the end of the experiment, you will be asked to complete a few short personality questionnaires.
Duration of the experiment
Approximately 30 minutes
Conditions for participation
  • You are 17 or older
  • You have normal eyesight or wear glasses/lenses to correct your eyesight
  • You are participating in this study on a computer screen (not using a smartphone or tablet)
